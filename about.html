“You are much stronger than you think you are. Trust me.” - Superman

Comic books have been a part of popular culture for nearly a century. As a relatively young medium of art, they have inspired people to help others, to be brave and bold, and to embrace peoples' uniqueness. The stories and characters have branched from their original paper format to other mediums like movies, television, and video games. This website is dedicated to connecting the iconic characters from comic books to their depictions in popular movies. To learn more about comic books or movies visit the links below.
